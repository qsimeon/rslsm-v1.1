Scientific Goal

First-ever imaging of consciousness across an entire vertebrate brain

Using voltage indicators and the rsLSM's high-speed volumetric imaging capabilities, we aim to capture neural activity dynamics across the entire zebrafish brain at millisecond timescales.

  • Whole-brain voltage imaging at kilohertz rates
  • Remote scanning eliminates sample motion artifacts
  • Optimized for genetically encoded voltage indicators
